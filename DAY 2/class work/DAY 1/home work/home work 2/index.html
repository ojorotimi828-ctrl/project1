Our Story

Traditional Taste, Modern Flair

Mercy Bite Cake and Pastries started with a simple passion: to bring high-quality, delicious baked goods to the people of Ore. What began as a small home kitchen has grown into a beloved local spot for celebration cakes and daily snacks.

We believe that every bite should be a memory. Whether it's our rich, moist chocolate cake or our flaky, savory meat pies, we put our heart into everything we bake.
